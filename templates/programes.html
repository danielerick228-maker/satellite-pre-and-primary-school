Academic programmes

At Satellite pre and primary school, we offer a rich and diverse curriculum designed to equip our learners with both academic excellence and real-world skills. Our key programmes include:

  • English language – Main medium of instruction focusing on speaking, reading, and writing fluently and confidently.
  • French language – Basic French to help learners become global citizens in a multilingual world.
  • Chinese language – Introductory Chinese for early advantage in international communication.
  • Computer studies – From basic skills to digital literacy; using technology creatively and responsibly.
  • Mathematics – Strong foundation in numeracy, logic, and problem solving.
  • Science – Encouraging curiosity through hands-on experiments to understand the world.
  • Civics and moral education – Instilling good manners, discipline, and social responsibility.
  • Physical education – Promoting fitness, teamwork, and healthy lifestyles through games and exercise.
  • Social studies – Understanding history, culture, geography, and civic responsibility.

Our goal: To provide holistic education that develops the mind, body, and character of every child.
